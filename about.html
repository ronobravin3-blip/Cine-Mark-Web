About Us

Cinemark Consult Limited is a CMA(K)-licensed Authorized Securities Dealer, providing regulated Fixed Income Trading, Eurobonds Execution, Research, and Advisory services.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be the preferred fixed income trading and advisory partner for institutional investors in East Africa.

Mission

To deliver disciplined execution, timely market intelligence, and bespoke advisory services that enable clients to optimize returns across fixed income markets.

Core Values

1. Professionalism

We conduct our business with discipline, competence, and respect, maintaining the highest standards of conduct, technical excellence, and institutional-grade service delivery at all times.

2. Integrity

We act honestly, ethically, and transparently in all our dealings, placing the interests of clients and the integrity of the capital markets above all else.

3. Best Execution

We are committed to delivering best execution for our clients by providing competitive pricing, timely trade execution, efficient settlement, and informed market access across fixed income markets.

4. Client Centricity

We place our clients at the center of everything we do, offering tailored solutions, responsive service, and objective advice aligned to their investment and treasury objectives.
